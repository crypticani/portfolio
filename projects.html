A Secure and Intelligent Data Sharing Scheme For UAV-Assisted Disaster rescue

  • Python, Machine Learning, Writing DQN models,Data preprocessing,Graph simulations

In this project, we apply a Deep Q-Learning (DQN) based approach to optimize cooperation between UAVs and ground vehicles during disaster rescue operations. Since neither UAVs nor vehicles have complete knowledge of the network environment, both agents learn optimal strategies through reinforcement learning. The UAV uses DQN to determine the best payment strategy for encouraging vehicle cooperation, while each ground vehicle learns its optimal AoCR (Amount of Computation Resource) strategy using a separate DQN model. CNN-based Q-value estimation, Îµ-greedy exploration, and mini-batch SGD updates enable both sides to adaptively improve decision-making and ensure secure, efficient, and intelligent data sharing in dynamic disaster scenarios.
